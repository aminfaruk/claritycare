Who We Are

Clarity Core Care is an NDIS registered provider built around one idea: support should fit your life, not the other way around. We work alongside participants, families, and support coordinators to deliver day-to-day care that's reliable, respectful, and shaped by what you actually want.

As an NDIS registered provider, Clarity Core Care is audited against the NDIS Practice Standards. That covers worker screening, incident management, complaints handling, and participant safeguarding. It's also the registration self, plan, and agency managed participants need to access our supports.

Your Information

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026. This policy explains how Clarity Core Care collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information.

1. Who we are

Clarity Core Care (ABN 70 694 991 912) is an NDIS support provider delivering day-to-day supports to participants across Australia. In this policy, "we", "us", and "our" refer to Clarity Core Care.

2. What information we collect

We collect personal information that is reasonably necessary to deliver your supports and meet our legal obligations. This can include:

  • Your name, contact details, date of birth, and address
  • NDIS participant number and plan details
  • Health, medical, and disability-related information relevant to your supports
  • Information about your goals, preferences, routines, and cultural needs
  • Next of kin, nominee, guardian, and emergency contact details
  • Records of our interactions, shift notes, and incident reports
  • Payment and billing information

3. How we collect it

We collect information directly from you wherever possible. In some cases we may also receive information from your support coordinator, plan manager, family member, guardian, nominee, the NDIA, or another health professional involved in your care, with your consent or where the law allows.

4. How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • Plan, schedule, and deliver your supports
  • Match you with suitable support workers
  • Communicate with you and your nominated contacts
  • Invoice and administer payments under the NDIS or other funding
  • Meet our legal, regulatory, and NDIS Practice Standards obligations
  • Respond to complaints, incidents, and feedback
  • Continuously improve the quality of our services

5. When we share information

We only share your personal information where you have given consent, where it is reasonably necessary to deliver your supports, or where we are required by law. This may include sharing with your support coordinator, plan manager, the NDIA, the NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission, health practitioners, or emergency services. We do not sell your information to third parties.

6. How we store and protect it

Your information is stored in secure systems with access limited to staff who need it to perform their roles. We use reasonable physical, electronic, and organisational safeguards to protect against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Records are retained for as long as required by law and NDIS Practice Standards.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Request a copy of your records
  • Ask us to correct information that is inaccurate or out of date
  • Withdraw consent for certain uses of your information
  • Make a complaint about how we handle your information

8. Cookies and website data

Our website uses minimal analytics and standard web server logs to understand how visitors use our site. We do not use your website browsing activity to identify you personally.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the date at the top showing when it was last reviewed.

10. Contact us

For any privacy questions, to access or correct your information, or to make a complaint, please contact us at info@claritycorecare.com.au or 0450 815 187. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at oaic.gov.au.

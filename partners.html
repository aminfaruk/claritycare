Referral Pathway

From first call to first shift.

A clear, predictable process so you know exactly where a referral sits at every stage.

01

Get in touch

Send the participant's plan details, goals, and preferred start date through our contact form, email, or phone.

02

Discovery call

A short call with you, and the participant or their family if appropriate, to clarify goals, routines, and preferences.

03

Service agreement

We send a clear, plain-language service agreement with line items, rates, and cancellation terms. Signed electronically.

04

Rostered & started

A worker matched to the participant's preferences is rostered, shift notes begin, and you're kept in the loop from day one.
